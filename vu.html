  1. WHO CAN APPLY FOR ADMISSION
    1. The applicants fulfill the basic admission requirements of a degree / diploma programs must possess the following documents:
      1. Meeting with the minimum prescribed admission requirements for the particular study program
      2. Valid CNIC / B Form number
      3. Valid Email address (Preferably applicant‚Äôs own)
      4. Cell Phone number (Preferably applicant‚Äôs own)
      5. Photographs of applicant (Passport Size with Blue Background)
      6. Scanned documents with clear readability
      7. Equivalence Certificate from IBCC and HEC in case the applicant having A / O Level Certificates and Foreign Degrees as applicable.
      8. Course Contents, original NOC from Previous Institute/University and original Incomplete Result Card (Only for the applicants who are seeking admission on the basis of credit transfer/course exemption through General Route based on the Incomplete Bachelor‚Äôs Result Card).
    2. Admissions to the Foreign or Dual National students will be offered after completing necessary codal formalities / confirmation from the respective offices (HEC, vetting agencies). Such applicants may visit VU website link https://www.vu.edu.pk/pages/AdmissionForeign.aspx for detailed information and necessary documentations to be provided to the University.
  2. INFORMATION FOR MERIT BASED PROGRAMS
    • The applicants seeking admission towards Biological Programs BS Bioinformatics, BS Biotechnology, BS Zoology Programs, Diploma in Bioinformatics & Diploma in Molecular Biology will be required to select Lab preferences as merit will be determined LAB Wise except MS Program.
    • The merit list of the successful candidates of Biological Science & MS Programs will be published at VU website on the prescribed dates as per Admission schedule.
    • Successful candidate will deposit fee within due date.
  3. GENERAL INFORMATION
    • Admissions will be opened as per the schedule given as above.
    • The admissions will not be processed to the result awaiting applicants on the basis of HOPE CERTIFICATE.
    • Admission Forms those filled or submitted after due date will not be accepted.
    • The documents must be uploaded in the Registration Interface as per given instructions.
    • Incomplete Admission Forms will be rejected, so carefully upload readable complete sets of documents to avoid any inconvenience.
    • The applicants are advised to enter his/her own and correct CNIC number(s) by filling Registration form during SIGN UP to avoid Rejection of admission form.
    • In case candidate is already enrolled at VU and his /her admission status is regular/in-active, he / she is required to firstly cancel the old admission before applying for new admission.

      • The applicant(s) shall deposit admission fee against one admission form only. In case, the applicant(s) submit fee against multiple programs then the University is fully authorized to cancel admission of one or all programme(s) and confiscate deposited fee.
      • Provision of correct information without concealing facts.
      • The University shall blacklist and confiscate the fee/degree, etc. at any time in case of mis-statement, false information found in admission form or bogus documents provided by the applicant.

  4. Course Exemption:
    1. General Route
      The applicants of the below category can apply for Course Exemption through general route:
      1. Applicant(s) intend to apply for Course Exemptions based on already qualified courses or Incomplete Degree/Diploma/Certificate from VU; subsequent to admission eligibility of the desired program; qualified within last five years.
      2. Applicants studying at any HEC recognized Uuniversity/Iinstitute and intend to transfer/exempt their qualified courses towards Virtual University of Pakistan.
      NOTE: The applicants applying for Course Exemptions through General Route are required to submit Course Exemption Proforma in Hard Copy filled at the time of filling admission form along with original NOC, Original Incomplete transcript issued by the concerned authority of the University/Institute and Course Content.
    2. Entry Route
      1. The applicants who have completed 14/16-years of Bachelor degree e.g. BA/B.Sc./B.Com, Associate Degree and BS degree programs may apply for admission through BS Entry Route. However, such applicants shall meet the basic admission eligibility criteria of the BS Program
      2. The Equivalence Committee of the University will evaluate the course exemptions cases based on the requisite qualification of the applicant.
      3. Credit Hours Transfer / Course Exemption policy/rules are given at https://www.vu.edu.pk/Admissions/TransferStudents.aspx
      4. Decision of the Equivalence Committee will be communicated to applicants via email.
